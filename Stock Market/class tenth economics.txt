- Gross National Income ->> The gross national income (GNI), previously known as gross national product (GNP), 
-> is the total domestic and foreign output claimed by residents of a country,
-> consisting of gross domestic product (GDP), plus factor incomes earned by foreign residents, minus income earned in the domestic economy by non-residents
-> Comparing GNI to GDP shows the degree to which a nation's GDP represents domestic or international activity.
